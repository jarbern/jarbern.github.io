E'tat Libre
D'Orange

E'tat is a French perfumery with a wicked sense of humor that shows through their website.

Designing a Shopify e-commerce site with Liquid themes required a combination of design skills and technical knowledge. Liquid is a templating language that allowed creating dynamic and customizable themes for the Shopify store. By using Liquid, a unique and engaging user experience was created while still maintaining the functionality and security of a Shopify site.

Another important aspect of designing the Shopify e-commerce site was implementing first-party cookie tracking with JavaScript. This allowed tracking user behavior on the website, such as which pages they visited and what products they added to their cart. This data was then used to optimize the user experience and improve conversions. However, it was important to comply with international and US cookie laws by providing clear and prominent information about the cookies being used on the site, and obtaining the user's consent before placing cookies on their device.

Finally, to gain valuable insights into the performance of the site, it was important to integrate the tags with Google Analytics. This allowed tracking important metrics such as website traffic, conversion rates, and revenue. Additionally, it allowed segmenting the data and gaining deeper insights into the behavior of different groups of users, and using that information to optimize the site and improve the user experience.
