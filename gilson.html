Gilson
Snowboards

Gilson Snowboards is a small indepedent manufacturer of unique Snowboards

Developing a website for Gilson Boards, a small independent snowboard manufacturer, required a combination of technical skills and marketing strategies. One of the key elements in developing the website was to implement Google Tag Manager. This enabled the tracking of website visitors' behavior and the collection of data that was used to optimize the website's performance. By installing Google Tag Manager, we were able to track visitor's clicks, scrolls, and form submissions, which were used to create a custom analytics dashboard that provided valuable insights into the website's performance.

Additionally, we also focused on creating a website that was responsive, user-friendly, and visually appealing. We also made sure that the website was optimized for search engines, which helped to increase its visibility and drive more traffic to the site. With this approach, we were able to create a website that not only looked great but also performed well and helped to increase the visibility and exposure of Gilson Boards.

Another important aspect of the solution was to create and manage YouTube bumper ads internationally. Bumper ads are short, non-skippable video ads that are shown before a video on YouTube. By creating and managing these ads, we were able to increase brand exposure and drive more traffic to the website. We targeted these ads to specific regions and demographics, which helped to increase the chances of reaching the right audience. We also used data from Google Tag Manager to optimize the ads and improve their performance creating a functioning data pipeline.
