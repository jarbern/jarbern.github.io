Cyber Security

A cybersecurity firm launching new products needs a facelift

Designing the blog section for a new cyber security firm was a challenging and rewarding experience. I utilized Wordpress as the framework for the blog, which provided a solid foundation for the development process. One of the key challenges I faced was managing the different development environments, including pushing updates to the staging and development environments for version control. This helped to ensure that the blog was fully functional and secure before being launched to the public.

One of the key features I implemented for the blog was content recommendations. This feature uses machine learning algorithms to recommend relevant articles to readers based on the content they have previously viewed. This helped to increase engagement and retention on the website, as readers were more likely to find content that was relevant to their interests.

I also made sure that the blog was responsive and optimized for mobile devices. As mobile usage continues to rise, it's important to ensure that websites are accessible and easy to use on smaller screens. By making the blog responsive, I ensured that readers could access the content on any device, which helped to increase engagement and improve the user experience.
