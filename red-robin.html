Red Robin
Burgers

A national chain with a hometown feel finding its place on Google.

One of the largest burger franchises in the country, Red Robin has over 500 locations across the nation. The problem faced by the company was how best to promote their B2B catering service. Specifically how bestto promote the new promotion for businesses to feed their employees during the long black friday and holiday shift

The platform used by Red Robin was WordPress. The goal was to drive awareness to a new product which had no legacy. The first step was creating a web pages that fit in the brand bible guidelines whilst keeping in mind the SEO keywords that were most important to the success of the campaign.

After creating the web pages and editing the metadata it was time to promote the new offerings outside of the website while Google's indexing spiders crawled and ranked the new pages. It was reccomended to include a Instagram campaign to call businesses to action in the weeks leading up to the promotion.

The pages and social media campaigns were ultimately successful in driving catering orders in the first quarter that the service ever existed. For specific keywords such as "black friday catering" and "catered burgers" the Red Robin pages reached the first page of google results in less than 2 month time.
